Portfolio Site Plan

Aurora Smith

WDD 131-Week 11

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site would be to showcase the projects that I have done over the last few years. I would focus on show case the skills that I have and what I have learned from the projects. And since this is my portfolio website, it should be unique to me.

Audience

The main people that would see this website are interested employers. They will want to see what I am like and what skills I have. They will be professional.

Dynamic elements

I will use Javascript to sort through my projects that will be designed like clickable cards. They will be sorted by skills. I will use .filter() and .map() to sort through the list of project objects I will create.
